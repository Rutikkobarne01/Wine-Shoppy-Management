DESCRIPTION

Overview

- A Tambda Rassa thali is a non-vegetarian meal that includes tambda rassa, a red curry from Kolhapur, and other dishes:
- Tambda rassa: A red curry made with meat stock and spices
- Sukka: A dish of mutton or chicken prepared with kala masala, which is made by charring onion and dried coconut over direct flame
- Pandhra rassa: A white-colored chicken gravy made with masala paste, mutton or chicken stock, and coconut milk
- Rice: Matar rice or steamed rice
- Roti or bhakri: A traditional flatbread
- Salad: Green salad

Benefits

- Fried chicken has some health benefits, but it's also high in calories and unhealthy fats, so it's best to eat it in moderation:
- Protein: Fried chicken is a good source of lean protein, which helps build muscle, maintain tissues, and support a healthy weight.
- Vitamins and minerals: Chicken contains vitamins and minerals that help with brain function.
- Immune system: Chicken has nutrients that can help strengthen your immune system.

Quality
